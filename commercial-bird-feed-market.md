<p><ul><li>Staple</li><li>Supplement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial bird feed market can be divided into two primary types: staple and supplement feeds. Staple feeds serve as the primary diet for birds, providing essential nutrients and energy for their overall health and well-being. These typically include grains, seeds, and pellets. In contrast, supplement feeds are designed to enhance the nutritional profile of the staple diet, offering additional vitamins, minerals, or specific ingredients like fruits and insects to support growth, breeding, or health during specific life stages.</p></p>
