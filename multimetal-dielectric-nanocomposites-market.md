<p><ul><li>High-k Multimetal Dielectric Nanocomposites</li><li>Low-k Multimetal Dielectric Nanocomposites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multimetal dielectric nanocomposites market is categorized into high-k and low-k types based on dielectric constant values. High-k multimetal dielectric nanocomposites feature elevated dielectric constants, enhancing capacitance and enabling miniaturization in electronic applications, particularly in capacitors and transistors. In contrast, low-k multimetal dielectric nanocomposites possess lower dielectric constants, reducing capacitance and diminishing signal crosstalk, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor devices and improving overall performance in high-speed electronics. Both types are pivotal in optimizing device efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
