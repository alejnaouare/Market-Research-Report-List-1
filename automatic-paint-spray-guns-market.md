<p><p>The automatic paint spray guns market is primarily categorized into pneumatic and electric spray guns. Pneumatic spray guns utilize compressed air to atomize and spray paint, offering precise control and a wide range of applications, especially for large-scale projects. Electric spray guns, on the other hand, rely on electric motors for paint application, providing ease of use and portability, making them ideal for smaller tasks or DIY projects. Both types are essential in various industries, ensuring efficient and high-quality coating finishes.</p></p>
