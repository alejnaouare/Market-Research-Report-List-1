The market can be segmented into three main types: Zener barriers limit voltage and current to prevent ignition, isolator barriers galvanically isolate the safe and hazardous areas while allowing signal transmission, and converter barriers convert signals for safe use in measurement and control applications. Each type plays a crucial role in protecting personnel and equipment in industries such as oil, gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
