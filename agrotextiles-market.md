<p><p>The agrotextiles market consists of various types, including woven, knitted, nonwoven, and others. Woven agrotextiles are made from interlaced fibers, providing strength and durability for applications like crop protection and soil erosion control. Knitted agrotextiles utilize loops of yarn, offering flexibility and elasticity, ideal for applications such as greenhouse covers. Nonwoven agrotextiles are produced by bonding fibers together, making them suitable for weed control and moisture retention. Other types may include specialty fabrics tailored for unique agricultural needs.</p></p>
