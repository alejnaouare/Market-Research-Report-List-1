<p><strong>The Tinning Lines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-dip Tinning Line</li><li>Electrolytic Tinning Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tinning lines are specialized manufacturing processes used for coating metal surfaces with a layer of tin to enhance corrosion resistance and solderability. Hot-dip tinning lines involve immersing steel or other metals in molten tin, creating a thick, durable coating ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electrolytic tinning lines use an electrochemical process to deposit a thinner, more uniform layer of tin, suitable for lighter applications such as food packaging. Both methods cater to diverse market needs in various industries.</p></p>
