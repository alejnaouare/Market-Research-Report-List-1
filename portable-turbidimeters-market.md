<p><p>The portable turbidimeter market is divided into high-level and low-level measurement types, catering to different application needs. High-level measurement turbidimeters are designed for environments with significantly turbid water, measuring turbidity levels ranging from 0 to 4,000 NTU, ideal for wastewater treatment and industrial applications. In contrast, low-level measurement turbidimeters focus on detecting subtle changes in water clarity, typically ranging from 0.01 to 100 NTU, making them suitable for environmental monitoring and drinking water quality assessments.</p></p>
