<p><p>The water treatment membrane material market comprises various types catering to specific filtration needs. Reverse osmosis membranes effectively remove impurities at the molecular level, ideal for desalination and wastewater treatment. Ultrafiltration membranes target larger particles, effectively removing bacteria and colloids. Microfiltration membranes filter out even larger particles and suspended solids, while nanofiltration membranes bridge the gap between reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration, selectively removing divalent ions and organic molecules. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring water quality and safety.</p></p>
