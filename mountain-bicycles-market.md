<p><p>Mountain bicycles can be broadly categorized into four market types: </p><p>1. **Rigid** bikes feature no suspension, providing direct connection to the trail, ideal for smooth surfaces.  </p><p>2. **Hardtail** models have front suspension only, offering a balance of performance and lightweight design for a mix of terrains.  </p><p>3. **Softail** combines elements of both rigid and full suspension, providing rear flexibility without excessive weight.  </p><p>4. **Full Suspension** bikes feature front and rear suspension, enhancing comfort and control on rough terrain, perfect for aggressive trails.</p></p>
