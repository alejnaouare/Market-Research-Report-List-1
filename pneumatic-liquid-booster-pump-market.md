<p><p>The pneumatic liquid booster pump market is segmented into "One Way" and "Two-way" types based on their design and functionality. One Way pumps are designed to move liquid in a single direction, making them ideal for applications requiring constant flow without back pressure. In contrast, Two-way pumps facilitate fluid movement in both directions, offering greater versatility for applications that require dynamic flow control. These distinctions influence their usage across various industries, including manufacturing and fluid transfer systems.</p></p>
