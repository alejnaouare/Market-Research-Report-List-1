<p><strong>The Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Image Sensor</li><li>CCD Image Sensor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The image sensor market comprises primarily three types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) image sensors, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) image sensors, and other specialized sensors. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for smartphones and consumer cameras. CCD sensors offer higher image quality and lower noise, often used in professional photography and scientific applications. Other sensors include specialty technologies like APS (Active Pixel Sensor) and global shutter sensors, catering to niche markets.</p></p>
