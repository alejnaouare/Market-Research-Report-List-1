<p><p>The food sterilizer market encompasses various types, each employing distinct methods to eliminate microorganisms and ensure food safety. Heat sterilizers use high temperatures to kill pathogens, while steam sterilizers utilize pressurized steam for enhanced effectiveness. Radiation sterilizers employ ionizing radiation to target microorganisms without heat. Chemical sterilizers use specific chemical agents for disinfection, and filtration sterilizers remove contaminants through physical barriers. Other methods may include ultraviolet light or ozone treatment, expanding the options available for food preservation and safety.</p></p>
