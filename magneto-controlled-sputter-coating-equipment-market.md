<p><p>Magneto-controlled sputter coating equipment encompasses three primary market types based on power sources: DC powered, RF powered, and MF powered systems. DC powered systems utilize direct current, suitable for conductive materials, providing effective and efficient coating processes. RF powered systems use radio frequency for non-conductive materials, enabling deeper penetration and uniform coating. MF powered systems operate at medium frequencies, balancing the advantages of both DC and RF techniques. Each type addresses specific application needs across various industries.</p></p>
