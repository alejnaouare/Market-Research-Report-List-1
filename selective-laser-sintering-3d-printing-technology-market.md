<p><p>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) is an advanced 3D printing technology that utilizes a laser beam to fuse powdered materials, typically plastics, metals, or ceramics, into solid structures. SLS is widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, healthcare, and consumer goods due to its ability to create intricate designs without the need for additional support structures.</p><p>The Selective Laser Sintering 3D Printing Technology Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
