<p><ul><li>Single-Feed Check Scanners</li><li>Multi-Feed Check Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The check scanner market is divided into two main types: single-feed and multi-feed check scanners. Single-feed check scanners allow users to process one check at a time, making them ideal for low-volume environments or businesses with limited check processing needs. In contrast, multi-feed check scanners can handle multiple checks simultaneously, making them suitable for high-volume operations such as banks and retail establishments. The choice between the two depends on the user's processing requirements and volume of transactions.</p></p>
