<p><p>The Switch-mode Power Supply (SMPS) market is divided into AC/DC and DC/DC categories. In contrast, DC/DC converters transform one level of direct current voltage to another, enabling efficient power management in various applications such as battery-powered devices and renewable energy systems. Both types emphasize efficiency, reliability, and compact design, driving widespread adoption across industries.</p></p>
