<p><strong>The Electrical Bone Growth Stimulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Type</li><li>Implanted Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical bone growth stimulators market is divided into two primary types: external and implanted. External stimulators are non-invasive devices placed outside the body, using electromagnetic fields or ultrasound to promote bone healing and regeneration. They are often used for fractures or fusion surgeries. Implanted stimulators, on the other hand, are surgically placed within the body and provide targeted electrical stimulation directly to the bone, enhancing healing at the site of injury or surgery, especially for non-union fractures and spinal fusion.</p></p>
