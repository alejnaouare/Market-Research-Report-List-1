<p><p>The aerosol therapy market primarily encompasses inhalers, nebulizers, and bronchodilators. Inhalers deliver medication directly to the lungs via metered doses, often used for asthma and COPD management. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist, suitable for patients needing higher doses or those unable to use inhalers effectively. Bronchodilators are key medications that relax airway muscles, improving airflow and alleviating symptoms. Together, these products provide crucial therapeutic options for respiratory conditions, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
