<p><strong>The Laser Interferometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne Laser Interferometer</li><li>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser interferometer market is primarily categorized into two types: homodyne and heterodyne interferometers. Homodyne laser interferometers utilize a single laser source for measurement, providing high precision in applications like surface profiling and displacement measurement due to their simplicity and sensitivity. In contrast, heterodyne laser interferometers employ two laser beams of slightly different frequencies, enabling enhanced measurement range and speed, making them ideal for dynamic applications such as vibration analysis and high-speed motion detection in various industries.</p></p>
