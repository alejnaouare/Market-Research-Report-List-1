<p><p>The cocoa products market encompasses several key types: cocoa powder, cocoa paste, cocoa butter, and cocoa beans. Cocoa powder is derived from roasted and ground beans, used primarily in baking and beverages. Cocoa paste, or liquor, is created from ground cocoa beans, serving as a base for chocolate production. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from beans, is essential for confectionery and cosmetics. Lastly, cocoa beans are the raw material, crucial for the entire cocoa supply chain, influencing flavors and quality.</p></p>
