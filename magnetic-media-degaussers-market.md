<p><p>Magnetic media degaussers are essential for securely erasing data from magnetic storage devices. Coil degaussers utilize electromagnetic coils to generate a strong magnetic field, effectively disrupting and erasing data. Capacitive discharge degaussers use stored electrical energy to create a swift magnetic pulse, achieving rapid data erasure. Permanent magnet degaussers employ high-strength permanent magnets to demagnetize media, offering a simpler, maintenance-free option. Each type serves specific needs, balancing effectiveness, speed, and operational complexity in data destruction.</p></p>
