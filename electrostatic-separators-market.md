<p><p>The electrostatic separators market is divided into two main types: dry and wet. Dry electrostatic separators utilize ambient air to separate particles based on their electrostatic charge, making them ideal for processing dry materials. In contrast, wet electrostatic separators operate in a liquid medium, allowing for the separation of particles in slurry forms, which is advantageous for mineral processing. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries, including mining, recycling, and material recovery, catering to specific separation requirements.</p></p>
