<p><p>The honey powder market comprises various types, primarily granulated honey and powdered honey. Granulated honey is produced by dehydrating liquid honey, resulting in coarse crystals that maintain the natural flavor and sweetness. It is often used as a sweetener or ingredient in baking. Powdered honey, on the other hand, is finely processed and has a smooth texture, making it ideal for instant beverages and seasoning. Both types offer versatile uses in culinary applications, health products, and natural sweeteners.</p></p>
