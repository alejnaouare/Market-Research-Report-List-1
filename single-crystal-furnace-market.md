<p><strong>The Single Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Crystal Furnace market includes two primary types: the Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ method involves the crystallization of materials by pulling a seed crystal from a molten solution, commonly used for silicon production. In contrast, the FZ method employs a localized melting of the material using a focused energy source, allowing for the growth of high-purity crystals without contamination. Both methods play a crucial role in semiconductor and materials science industries.</p></p>
