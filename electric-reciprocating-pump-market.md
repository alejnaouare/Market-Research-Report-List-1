<p><p>The electric reciprocating pump market features three main types: piston pumps, plunger pumps, and diaphragm pumps. Piston pumps utilize a moving piston to create pressure, making them suitable for various fluids. Plunger pumps, similar to piston pumps but with a higher pressure capability, use a plunger for efficient liquid transfer, often in demanding applications. Diaphragm pumps employ a flexible diaphragm to create a pumping action, ideal for corrosive or sensitive liquids, ensuring safe and precise fluid handling across diverse industries.</p></p>
