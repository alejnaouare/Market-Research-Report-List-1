<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Technology Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic Wave Technology Biosensors are analytical devices that utilize acoustic waves to detect biological interactions. The Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor measures mass changes on its surface by monitoring frequency shifts, making it ideal for detecting biomolecular interactions with high sensitivity. Conversely, Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensors use acoustic waves traveling along the surface of a piezoelectric material, providing real-time monitoring of biological events and enabling applications in diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety. Both technologies are prized for their specificity and responsiveness.</p></p>
