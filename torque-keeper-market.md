<p><strong>The Torque Keeper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Type</li><li>Shear Pin Type</li><li>Roller Release Detent Type</li><li>Magnetic Torque Limiter Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Torque Keepers are devices designed to limit torque in mechanical systems, ensuring safety and performance. The Friction Type relies on friction surfaces to slip and prevent over-torque. The Shear Pin Type uses a specific pin that breaks under excessive torque, protecting components. The Roller Release Detent Type employs rollers that disengage under overload, allowing movement without damage. Lastly, the Magnetic Torque Limiter Type utilizes magnetic fields to disconnect drive elements, offering a non-contact method to manage torque levels efficiently.</p></p>
