<p><ul><li>Metered Dose Inhaler (MDI)</li><li>Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital inhalers market comprises primarily two types: Metered Dose Inhalers (MDI) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPI). MDIs deliver a specific dose of medication in aerosol form, offering precise control over dosage and ease of use. They often include smart technology for tracking usage and adherence. DPIs, on the other hand, require patients to inhale a dry powder medication deeply, relying on the user's breath for activation. Both types are increasingly integrating digital features to enhance patient engagement and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
