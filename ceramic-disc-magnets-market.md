<p><p>The ceramic disc magnets market is divided into hard and soft ceramic disc magnets. Hard ceramic disc magnets, often made from strontium ferrite or barium ferrite, exhibit high magnetic strength, making them ideal for permanent applications in motors, loudspeakers, and magnets for various devices. In contrast, soft ceramic disc magnets have lower remanence and are used in applications requiring temporary magnetic fields, such as magnetic shielding and certain electronic devices. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
