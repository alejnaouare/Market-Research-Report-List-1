<p><p>The coding equipment market is categorized into several types, each serving distinct printing needs. Continuous Ink Jet (CIJ) systems provide high-speed, continuous marking for various surfaces. Thermal Ink Jet (TIJ) technology is used for precise, high-resolution printing, often in smaller batches. Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) employs heat to transfer ink from a ribbon to packaging. Laser coding offers permanent marking with high precision on diverse materials. Other methods include impact printing and dot matrix, catering to specialized requirements across industries.</p></p>
