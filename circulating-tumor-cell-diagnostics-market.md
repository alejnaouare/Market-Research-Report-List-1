<p><p>The Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) diagnostics market encompasses three key types: CTC enrichment, CTC detection, and CTC analysis. CTC enrichment involves isolating tumor cells from blood, enhancing the sensitivity of subsequent testing. CTC detection focuses on identifying these isolated cells using various techniques, such as molecular assays. Finally, CTC analysis involves studying the characteristics and behavior of the detected cells to provide insights into tumor characteristics, treatment response, and disease progression, thus aiding personalized cancer management.</p></p>
