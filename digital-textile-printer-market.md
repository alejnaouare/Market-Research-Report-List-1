<p><p>The digital textile printer market comprises various printing technologies, primarily sublimation inkjet printing and direct-to-garment (DTG) printing. Sublimation inkjet printing involves transferring dye onto fabric using heat, ideal for synthetic materials, producing vibrant colors and sharp images. In contrast, DTG printing applies ink directly onto garments, allowing for intricate designs on a variety of fabrics, especially cotton. Both methods cater to diverse applications in fashion, home textiles, and promotional products, offering flexibility and customization in textile manufacturing.</p></p>
