<p><strong>The Seawater Desalination Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis System</li><li>Distillation System</li><li>Reverse Osmosis Combined With Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater desalination device market comprises three main types: Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems, Distillation systems, and a combination of both. RO systems use a semi-permeable membrane to separate salt and impurities from seawater, making it a widely adopted and energy-efficient method. Distillation systems, on the other hand, involve heating seawater to create steam, then condensing it back into liquid, effectively removing salts. The combined approach leverages both methods to optimize efficiency and improve water quality for varied applications.</p></p>
