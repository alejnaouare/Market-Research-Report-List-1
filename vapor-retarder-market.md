<p><p>The vapor retarder market is categorized based on permeability ratings, which measure the material's ability to allow moisture to pass through. Less than 0.1 Perm products are classified as impermeable, ideal for high-moisture environments. The 0.1 Perm to 1.0 Perm range offers moderate resistance, suitable for diverse applications where some moisture control is needed. The 1.0 Perm to 10 Perm category includes vapor-permeable materials, allowing for greater moisture transmission, making them suitable for environments requiring breathability while controlling condensation.</p></p>
