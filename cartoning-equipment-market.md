<p><p>Cartoning equipment is categorized into horizontal and vertical markets based on their design and functionality. Horizontal cartoning machines operate by placing products in a horizontal orientation, allowing for efficient loading and high-speed operations, ideal for large-scale packaging. In contrast, vertical cartoning equipment positions products vertically, which suits smaller or lighter items and often allows for more compact designs. Both types are essential in the packaging industry, catering to different product dimensions and production requirements.</p></p>
