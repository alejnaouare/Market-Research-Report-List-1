<p><p>The electrostatic fieldmeter market is divided into two main types: non-contacting and contacting devices. Non-contacting fieldmeters measure electric fields from a distance, making them useful for assessing static charges on surfaces without physical interference. In contrast, contacting fieldmeters require direct contact with the charged object to measure the electric field strength. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, like electronics and manufacturing, helping to manage and mitigate electrostatic discharge and ensure safety in operations involving static electricity.</p></p>
