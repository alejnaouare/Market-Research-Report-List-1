<p><p>NIR-SWIR imaging cameras are categorized into area scan and line scan types, each suited for different applications. Area scan cameras capture an entire image simultaneously, making them ideal for capturing static scenes or objects in real-time. They are commonly used in agricultural monitoring and quality control. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, excelling in high-speed applications like conveyor belt inspection and industrial automation, where continuous imaging is crucial for monitoring moving objects.</p></p>
